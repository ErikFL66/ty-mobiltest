WHAT WE ARE UP TO LATELY

For the last couple of months we've been getting ready to roll out our second album, so we have been really busy raking photos making stickers and other cool stuff. We have also been filming a couple of music videos that you all can look forward to. One of them was filmed in out friends living room as we held a secret house show filming session (little sneak peak to the right). We've also been getting to know Lau really well in a musical way and we're very excited to show of the new band dynamic in a live environment.

In the process of getting comfortable in out new band constellation we've taking a trip back to femø where we recorded and rehearsed som of the songs from our debut album. It's been great fun and it felt great to be back and take a break in a more secluded space surrounded by music, coffee and good friends.

MUSIC WE LISTENED TO

SPEAK DAGGERS - ELIAS RØNNENFELT

Vitus is going to play a couple of shows with Elias this year so this album (and all his other releases) have been in heavy rotation lately. We have loved his work for a very long time now, both solo and with iceage and this one is not an exception.

SO MUCH COUNTRY 'TILL WE GET THERE
- WESTSIDE COWBOY

WESTSIIIIDE COWBOYYYY! (if you know you know). They're really great and an incredible force of a band on stage. We had the fortune of seeing them live here in copenhagen last year and they were really great, so when this album dropped we of course had to give it a listen. And of course it's great.

GLASFISK - ECHO, BRAVO

Friends of the band Echo,Bravo recently released this gem of an album. It's captures what s going on right now in the underground alternative music scene in Copenhagen. Get on it it's great we swear!

ABOUT TENDER YOUTH

Tender Youth is an avant-garde/post-punk band based in Copenhagen, Denmark. The quartet was formed in 2020 and released their debut album 'In The Hands of Broken Bonds' in the spring of 2023. An album that garnered great reviews in several Danish media outlets like Gaffa, Soundvenue, and Undertoner for Tender Youth's newfound rock sound. It has been possible to experience Tender Youth's sound at the band's high-intensity and critically acclaimed live shows, seen at festivals like Roskilde Festival 2023 and Spot Festival 2023. A stepping stone for 2024, where the band is playing its first international festivals

TOUR DATES

OPERAEN

CHRISTIANIA, DENMARK

In the spring of 2023, Tender Youth released their debut album "In the Hands of Broken Bonds", which received rave reviews in several Danish media outlets. Now, the critically acclaimed rock album is finally coming out on vinyl in early March. The band is celebrating with a unique release party at the Opera House in Christiania on March 16. To make this evening truly special, Tender Youth will play two concerts in one night. The first concert will be an intimate experience, where the band will perform a set they have never performed before: a fully acoustic set with brand new interpretations of selected songs from the album. After that, Tender Youth will play an energetic, danceable, and noisy set like you've never seen them before—and maybe Tender Youth will even premiere some brand new material. This concert will be the first time you can buy Tender Youth's brand new vinyl physically, so don't miss out on this special evening.

SUPERSONIC

PARIS, FRANCE

OPENING FOR SPRIAL DRIVE

“Visions In Bloom” - with the second studio album released on March 11th 2022, Spiral Drive is back on national and international festival and club stages. After the success of the debut album “Unity” in 2019, Spiral Drive once again shows the ability to homogeneously merge psychedelic rock, alternative rock, psych pop, grunge and indie rock. “Rock for the masses, rock for the discerning, why aren't they more famous?» - Reverb is for Lovers, 2022 This is just one example of numerous enthusiastic national and international reviews, all of which have a common denominator: fresh neo-psych rock, songs that leave plenty of room for improvisation and jams at concerts - and you have to experience it live! For the production Raphael teamed up with his friend Manuel Renner to realize his visions in full bloom. Brutal riffs encounter deep psychedelic soundscapes of comforting analogue warmth.

DELTA

ROSKILDE, DENMARK

DELTA er et festivalforløb for unge eventmagere i alderen 13 - 19 år, der går til festival, mens andre går til fodbold. Og samtidig er DELTA er en musikfestival i Roskilde - skabt til unge fra unge. I DELTA lærer du en masse om booking af musik, markedsføring, pre-produktion og afvikling - alt det, der skal til for at lave et godt musikarrangement.
